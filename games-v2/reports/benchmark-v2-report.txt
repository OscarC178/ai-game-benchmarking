KEY FINDINGS
------------
1. GLM-5 writes best specs: 0% DNFs. Other planners 5-12.5%.
   GLM-5 specs are builder-agnostic — even Mini benefits.

2. GPT-5.4 Mini is catastrophic: 24/50 DNFs (48%). All 10 "GPT-5.4
   spec DNFs" in r2 came from Mini. Flagship GPT-5.4: 0 DNFs.

3. Spec writer > builder: GLM-5 specs → 0 DNFs with any builder.
   Sonnet (V1 hero) scored 9.34 in V2 — worst non-Mini builder.

4. No specs is mid-table, not disaster: 68/70 zero-error builds
   (more than GPT-5.4 specs: 59/70). Generic > broken ambitious.

5. Pac-Man breaks everything: avg 9.20. Direction queuing and
   collision grids defeat AI implementations consistently.

6. Sonnet dropped from V1 hero to mid-table: V1 tested raw
   capability; V2 tests spec-robustness. Sonnet executes well
   but isn't spec-robust.
